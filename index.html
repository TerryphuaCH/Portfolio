A summary about myself

My name is Phua Cheng Hong Terry from Fuchun Secondary School. I am kind and cordial to my classmates and the people around me. I will always lend a helping hand to people who need help, be it academically or other areas. I am a student councillor and camp instructor in school. I was also selected to participate in events such as a Closed-door Dialogue with PM Lee Hsien Loong, National School Games as a price steward and an organiser for my school camp. I am able to carry out tasks independently with minimal supervision and produce excellent results. I am receptive to feedback and also show willingness to work on areas where I can improve on.
